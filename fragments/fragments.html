CMOA Fragments

Welcome to the interactive exhibition "CMOA Fragments," which will allow you to explore the collection of the Carnegie Museum of Art in an immersive way, regardless of your geographical location. Within this exhibition, you will have the opportunity to view a selection of artworks from different eras and cultures. Moreover, you can actively engage with the exhibition by choosing your preferred classification, arrangement, and be pleasantly surprised by randomly displayed artworks. Through this project, our aim is to enhance the democratization, dissemination, and promotion of access to art.

How does it work?

Choose from the "Classification" dropdown menu one of over 40 classifications to explore the diversity.

Initially, the artworks are displayed in a predefined order. However, by clicking the "Randomize" button, you can view artworks arranged randomly.

Finally, you can select between two arrangements: "Spaced" and "Glued." The "Spaced" arrangement will display the artworks with more spacing between them, while the "Glued" arrangement will present them in a more closely connected manner.

Enjoy the CMOA Fragments visualization and discover the richness of culture from around the world.
