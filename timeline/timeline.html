CMOA Timeline

This is our Artist Timeline page. In this page you'll be able to find many different artists from many countries, all included in the Carnegie Museum of Art's database.

In this visualization, simply choose one artist of your preference and see all of their work sorted by date. You'll be also given a brief biografy about the artist, extracted from online articles. Note that there are also works of art from unknown artists from many different countries. These are works that don't have their autor in the CMOA database, but are still interesting to understand the trends over time and in different countries.

Feel free to look at all the artist's works and feel the evolution and change of their art over the year!
